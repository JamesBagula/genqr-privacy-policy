Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 5, 2025

GenQR is a Chrome Extension designed to generate QR codes from URLs without collecting any personally identifiable information (PII) or transmitting data to external servers.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any of the following:

  • Personal data such as name, email address, or location
  • Browsing history
  • Usage analytics
  • IP address or device identifiers
  • QR code content

2. Local Functionality

All QR code generation and decoding happens entirely within your browser. No data is sent to any remote server or third-party API. This ensures your privacy and security at all times.

3. Cookies and Tracking

This extension does not use cookies or any form of tracking technologies.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer user data to third parties. In fact, we do not have access to any data to begin with.

5. Third-Party Services

GenQR does not integrate with any third-party services or platforms that collect data.

6. Children's Privacy

This extension is safe for all age groups and does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13 years of age.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy in the future (unlikely, given the nature of the extension), they will be posted here with an updated effective date.
